The Problem

Discovery is Broken

Music discovery has shifted to social platforms, but the journey from "I like this song" to "I'm a fan" is broken. Listening has become passive, algorithmic, and disconnected from artist identity.

The Core Insight

Spotify excels at helping you find songs. But finding a song isn't the same as finding an artist. The listener-to-fan conversion funnel has no designed experience.

Key Pain Points

  • Shallow listening: Gen Z's listening is passive, nostalgic, and algorithm-driven‚Äîfans aren't building deep connections with artists
  • Discovery drop-off: After hearing a song on TikTok, most listeners never explore the artist's catalog
  • Artist cold-start: 100,000+ new tracks daily means emerging artists face impossible competition for visibility
  • Algorithm opacity: Users don't understand why they're recommended certain music, reducing trust and engagement

Sources: MIDiA Research, Spotify for Artists, Deloitte Digital Media Trends 2024, Record of the Day Gen Z Study

How It Works

When a listener shows high engagement signals (repeated plays, saves, full listens), Pathways activates on the artist profile‚Äîtransforming a static page into a guided journey of discovery with progress tracking.

Design Principles

  • Progressive disclosure: Works without interaction, but rewards those who want to go deeper
  • Transparent intelligence: Show users what the algorithm is doing and why
  • Context over catalog: Curate with meaning, not just popularity metrics
