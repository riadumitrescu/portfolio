The Opportunity

The games are world-class.
Discovery can be too.

Apple Arcade has the most thoughtfully curated game library on any platform. Playground explores how a mood-first, editorially-rich discovery layer could help even more players find the games they'll love.

🎭

Mood-First Discovery

What if Arcade opened with how you feel? A mood-based entry point — Chill, Thrill, Create, Explore — could match players to the right game faster and build intent before browsing.

Ref: Nielsen Norman Group — Intent-Based Navigation
✍️

Editorial Storytelling

Apple TV+ has cinematic trailers and editorial features. What if Arcade games got that same treatment? Stories about the worlds, the makers, the craft behind the games.

Ref: Apple TV+ Editorial Model
👥

Social-Lite Signals

Lightweight social cues — "2 friends played this week" or "trending in your circle" — could help surface gems through trust and real connections, not just algorithms.

Ref: Social Proof in Digital Experiences — Cialdini
🌟

Dynamic Home

A home screen that evolves — fresh picks by mood, time of day, seasonal collections — gives players a reason to check back and discover something new every session.

Ref: Spotify Discover Weekly Success Model
🎮

Deeper Matching

Beyond genre tags: what if recommendations understood the feeling of a game? Calm vs. tense, quick vs. deep, solo vs. social — attributes that match how people actually choose.

Ref: Steam Tags + Mood Taxonomy Research
🔄

Return Momentum

Smart nudges — "You left off here" or "New game from a studio you loved" — could build a natural return habit that keeps the library feeling fresh and alive.

Ref: Retention UX — Reforge Growth Series

Game pages with screenshots — informative, but the craft and stories behind these games deserve more room to shine

Implementation

How it could come to life

A phased approach — from defining the mood framework through polished, testable prototypes ready for stakeholder review.

🎭
Phase 1

Mood Framework

Define the mood taxonomy (Chill, Thrill, Create, Explore), map the game library, and prototype the mood-selection interaction.

Foundation
✍️
Phase 2

Editorial Stories

Design the story card system — rich visual templates, editorial format, and how stories refresh weekly. Usability test with players.

Content Layer
👥
Phase 3

Social Signals

Design the lightweight social layer — friends activity, trending badges, and "played by people like you" nudges that create community.

Social Layer
🚀
Phase 4

Polish & Present

High-fidelity prototypes in Figma, usability test findings, motion design polish, and a stakeholder-ready presentation.
